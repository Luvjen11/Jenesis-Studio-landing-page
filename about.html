Flute Playing

I started playing the flute in the first year of middle school and I Have to admit I hated it at first. I wanted to learn how to play the guitar or the piano but by the end of the second year I began to feel highly sad. That was because the flute I was using belonged to the school so I knew that the year after I would've had to return it back to the school. So I started crying out loud to my mum to get me a flute. So after months I convinced her to do so. Since I wasn't sure if she would have bought it for me in the main time I registered into the Band of my city because I knew that they would've given me a flute for free if I played with them. So that's how I entered the "Banda Musicale di Villadose". I currently play with them now and I intend to attend a conservatory soon to get a qualification too.
